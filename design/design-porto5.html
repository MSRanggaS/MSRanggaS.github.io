Olahkarsa PROPER (Internship Project)

Olahkarsa PROPER is an application product from PT Olahkarsa Inovasi Indonesia where I was an intern and was responsible for the UI/UX Design division. The application will assist the company in conducting a self-assessment as an effort to prepare for the readiness of the proper assessment held by the Ministry of Environment and Forestry.
